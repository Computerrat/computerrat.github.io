Chapter 1

"Captain, I'd be honored to assist on this voyage."

"Glad to hear," says the Captain. "Come with me and I will show you some of the charts I've been working on. I have a theory that it is possible to end up in the East by actually sailing West around the world."

As the two of you walk down to the ship, the Captain tells you that everything he has told you is in confidence, since he isn't sure how the crew will respond.

"When are you planning to set sail?" you ask.

"How long will it take you to get prepared?" the Captain responds, "I had planned to sail at dawn, but since the tide is favoring us now, we could go ahead. You're the navigator...let this be the first of many decisions you make!"
