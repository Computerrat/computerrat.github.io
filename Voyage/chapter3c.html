Chapter 3

You quickly and quietly make your way down to the ship. "Captain, I'm glad you're here. I need to speak to you immediately. It's very urgent!"

"What's the problem? Surely you haven't changed your mind..."

"No, sir!", you respond, "I'm just not sure we should set sail..."

The Captain gives you a funny look. "Why the change of heart?"

As you start to tell the Captain about the conversation you overheard, he stops you. "I'm glad to see where your loyalties lie. That conversation was meant to be overheard by you as a test. Now I can tell you the true purpose of this voyage. Yes, I do have a theory--but not to sail to the Spice Islands, but rather to find the legendary Cibola! You are familiar with this legend?"

"Of course," you reply. "Every seaman knows about the fabled seven cities of gold! But what makes you think you can find the location after so many men have failed?!?"

"Now, now I can't tell you all of my secrets now, can I?" laughs the Captain. "What do you say? Are you still with me?"
