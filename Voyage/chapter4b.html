Chapter 4

"I've never been to this island before and I'd like a chance to explore," you tell the Captain.

"Very well, on your way!"

As you are making your way into the dense foliage, you think you hear some strange sounds, almost like someone singing. You ask the other sailors if it was one of them, and they give you a funny look. "We thought it was you!" Suddenly you all start looking around because you hear a rustling in the leaves, but there is no wind.You feel a sharp prick on the back of your neck, almost like a mosquito bite, and then everything goes black. As you start to wake up, you think you must have a fever because you are so hot. Then you begin to think you smell something cooking. When you try to move, you realize that your feet and hands are bound. You open your eyes and see that your shipmates are all in the same condition as you; and then you realize that the uninhabited island you had gone to explore was actually inhabited by a tribe of cannibals!
