Chapter 3

You continue to study your charts and make your decision. "Captain, if we continue on course we should be able to make land within the next three days, weather permitting."

"Very well, we'll keep on course, for now."

Suddenly, one of the crew yells, "Land ho!"

"Good job," says the The Captain. "Very well done! Since this island is the last place to gather supplies before the uncharted waters, do you want to go with some of the crew members to find provisions or would you rather stay on board to plot the next part of the voyage?"
