Introduction

The year is 1519. Your reputation as a navigator is well-known in nautical circles. The captain of the Spanish carrack Nao Victoria has recruited you to be the navigator for his next voyage. He talks to you about this voyage; explaining that he has a new route to get to the Spice Islands, but a lot of this new route goes through uncharted waters. He is well aware of your experiences, and tells you that he is counting on you to help him on this quest. Do you agree to help him?
