Chapter 2

"Even with the tide favoring us now, I think we should sail at dawn."

"Very well," says the Captain, "Prepare to sail at dawn."

You gather some last minute supplies from town, and then decide to turn in early. You are jarred awake by the sound of thunder. A flash of lightning illuminates the night sky. "I hope this blows over soon," you think to yourself. You decide to go on down to the docks and get settled on board the ship before it sets sail. As you are walking along the docks, you hear voices. You stop and listen as you think you recognize one of the voices. You overhear several of your new ship mates discussing how they are going to stage a mutiny and throw the Captain overboard so that they can keep all of the treasure for themselves. Now you're not sure what to do. After all, the Captain hadn't mentioned anything about treasure when he spoke to you yesterday. Should you go see the Captain and tell him what you heard? Or should you just wait and see what happens once the the ship sets sail?
